What is Infotsav all abt?

Infotsav is the annual Technology and Management Festival of the institute. It celebrates the spirit of the institute, embarked by its fight for creativity, excellence and being leader. The festival witnesses big corporate names as sponsors and big institutes as participants, ranging from IITK, IITB, NITIE, IITD etc. The excellence has always been rewarded, and the witness is huge prize money that is at stake every year. Infotsav'06 had a special feature of organizing "e-Governors Summit'06: Citizen Centric PPP Approach".

Dates
