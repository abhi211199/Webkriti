speakFight




















About...

It is an event intended to bring the sensible, relevant and original inner orator of those people who dare saying the truth but carry an optimistic approach to every state of affairs. Based on extemporaneous speaking, event provides a chance for students to give a worthwhile and fertile group discussion on the given topics under a time limit.

Rules...

  • The participant(s) must be a bonafide student of an engineering or management institute of India.
  • Individual participation will be there.
  • A group of 5 participants will be given two topics on the spot, one after the other, to have a discussion on.
