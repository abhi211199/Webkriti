Coding Wars
















About...

If you are confident enough that you have mastered the ultimate mind sport, then come solve mind blowing problems and play the game of sports in Coding Wars.It is a challenging stage, nail biting one! Step up your coding skills and gather to compete for the crown!

Rules...

  • The participant(s) must be a bonafide student of an engineering or management institute of India.
  • There will be two rounds for the event. Participants will be tested on the basis of their skills in competitive programming.
  • The top contestants from the first round will qualify for the second round.The second round will be an onsite event. The winner and runner up of the contest will be given the prize money.
